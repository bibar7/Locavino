Our Story

We are Jarrod Jabre (far right) and Justin Wallace (center): two experienced restaurant, wine, and beer managers as well as your fellow Silver Spring locals. We are proud to present Locavino, a new wine and beer establishment in Downtown Silver Spring! In addition to beer, cider, and wine, Locavino offers a robust food menu, as well as retail alcoholic beverages. The name Locavino (blatantly stolen from the word "locavore") reflects our mission to shine a spotlight on local beverages in the context of the global wine and beer market. It is important to us that Locavino serve not only as a gathering place for our neighborhood but also as a force for good within our beloved community, which for us includes the use of sustainable sourcing wherever possible.
